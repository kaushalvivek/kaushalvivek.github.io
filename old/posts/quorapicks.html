Favourite Bookmarks from Quora

Quora is awesome. It's different. In a world where a thousand apps are trying to do the exact same thing, Quora manages to stand out, create a niche space for itself. These are some of my favourite answers on Quora (my bookmarks as on 5th October, 2017). Shoutout to the writers.

What do you wish you knew in your 30's?
What are the worst mistakes people make in job interviews?
What is your most cynical opinion?
What is the creepiest thing any AI has done so far?
What are some smart things that lazy people do?
How do some people enjoy so much and yet score well?
What have you heard accidentally after someone thought their phone call with you was over?
What is the life people should not live?
Have you ever known someone who was too perfect?
I am feeling very low. Can you say something which can instantly uplift me?
What are some amazing replies you got in your life?
Why should I insist on living my pathetic life?
What are the different types of friendships?
Has anyone surprised you after their death, e.g. receiving an organ from a donor, a sizeable inheritance, or a visit from a friendly ghost?
What is the strangest sorting algorithm?
This is Julian.

It's amazing how Quora enables anyone to inspire and affect millions through something as simple as words. With all of our Social Networks and Search Engines, no one ever felt the need for a Quora until we got one.
